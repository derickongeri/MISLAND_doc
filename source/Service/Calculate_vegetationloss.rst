Calculate Vegetation Loss/Gain indicators
==========================================

To compute vegetation loss/gain on the service platform, click on the |veglossicon| option on the service menu bar and follow these simple steps:

1. Select the |veglossbutton| for the layer settings pannel to pop-up as shown below.

.. figure:: ../_static/Images/Service/veg1.png
    :width: 219
    :align: center
    :height: 211
    :alt: Vegetation gain/loss service
    :figclass: align-center

    Vegetatio loss/gain pop-up

2. Select the data source of your choice and the reporting year and click on the |submit| button to compute vegetation loss and gain. The results hsould be as shown below.

.. figure:: ../_static/Images/Service/veg2.png
    :width: 889
    :align: center
    :height: 422
    :alt: select country
    :figclass: align-center

    Vegetation loss/gain results
