Calculate Coastal Erosion
===========================

1. Select the |coastalbtn| on the service menu-bar and click on the |coastaloption| for the layers settings pop-up to appear as shown below. 

2. Select the coastal vulnerability index, or any of the coastal vulnerability index factor map.

.. figure:: ../_static/Images/Service/coastal2.png
    :width: 256
    :align: center
    :height: 436
    :alt: ilswe
    :figclass: align-center


3. Chose the reporting year and click on the |submit| button to get the results as shown below

.. figure:: ../_static/Images/Service/coastal3.png
    :width: 859
    :align: center
    :height: 442
    :alt: ilswe
    :figclass: align-center
